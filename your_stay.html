Accomodation

Place

Slunečna Hut
Nová Ves 84, 795 01 Dolní Moravice
Czech Republic

Description

Slunečna Hut is located in the heart of Jeseníky mountain range surrounded by forests, skiing slopes, trekking routes, etc. It is a recreational area with a main building and five small huts. There is enough space for 23 people in the main building (3 double rooms, 3 three-bedded rooms and one big room for 8 people), while the small huts have 4 beds each. The dining-room, sanitary facilities and other common rooms are in the main building.

How to get there

The nearest international airports where you can fly to are Prague (CZ) and Brno (CZ), while Vienna (AUT) and Bratislava (SK) also have some good connections. From there, you can take a train to small cities where we can pick you up: Rýmařov or Bruntál.

If you are travelling by car, it is possible to park for free close to our accommodation.

If you let us know your options, we will try to help with transportation individually. Few days before the event, we will connect participants with similar transport options and make it as easy as possible for everybody.
