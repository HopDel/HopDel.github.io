Animal Instinct

Move. Play. Discover.

About

Animal Instict is a 5 day event based on discovering activities that bring joy without regard of a momentaliry discomfort. All these types of activities will be present on the event - from guided cold water dive, movement training, hiking or running, music, games, campfires and others. The event is held in mountain range in Czechia and its purpose is to broaden your horizons, push your limits and allow you to discover and connect with yourself.

After 12 years of experience with organising sport-events / camps in a small village of "Miroslav" in Czech Republic, I decided to develop this thought of adventure for a wider circle of people.

We started two years ago with a few friends and created this event for Czech people. We tried to share our accumulated energy and life experience in only five days with everybody who would like to work on themselves and have fun at the same time. We would like to connect with people who have the same enthusiasm into bigger group. We want you to work hard, laugh from your heart, think and be united with nature.

Miroslav Sokol, Founder of the Animal Instinct event

Other Information

The capacity is limited to 20 participants.

There are two levels on which you can participate. You can choose the level with option to switch in case you would underestimate or overestimate yourself. Level 1 will be mentored by Lia Tomat and Pavel Milička, Level 3 will be mentored by Miroslav Sokol. The description of individual levels and the idea behind them be found in section Levels.

What to bring: Outdoor and indoor clothes (that can get dirty or destroyed), slippers, running shoes (for forest, rocks..), head-light, scarf, notebook, pen, bottle, rain coat, swimsuit, towel, small backpack, pocket knife, anything else you normally use.
