About

Animal Instict is a 5 day event based on discovering activities that bring joy without regard of a momentaliry discomfort. All these types of activities will be present on the event - from guided cold water dive, movement training, hiking or running, music, games, campfires and others. The event is held in mountain range in Czechia and its purpose is to broaden your horizons, push your limits and allow you to discover and connect with yourself.

What

Accomodation and five meals per day is included along with all the fun activities, interesting tasks, rituals, campfires, rhytmical improvisation and reasonable amount of movement. For the physical activities, you can choose from two levels, level 1 is suitable for begginers, whereas level 3 is suited for people who have movement or sport as an important part of their life and will be a bit more demanding. Majority of the activities will be non-seperated, so you do not have to worry about brining a friend who is on a different physical level.

The event is limited to 20 people to ensure you can get to know each other during the event and due to the accomodation limitation.
